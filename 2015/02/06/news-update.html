6th February 2015 - Meath Championship Finals 2015
A great nights play at Kildalkey with the conclusion of two weeks qualifying for the county championship! The large crowd provided support and non stop applause for the more than deserving players, who put on a great show in their matches. Thanks are due to Roberta Hamilton who once again ran an excellent tournament, ably assisted by Seamus Halpin. Well done to members of the MBA board who helped out on all match nights too!

Congratulations to Navan Badminton Club players who entered in great numbers reaching many semi-finals and also many finalists.

  • Grade 7 - Ladies Winners Shanti Petticrew & Ciara Boylan
  • Grade 7 - Mens Winners Kyle Dunphy & Gerard Morgan
  • Grade 7 - Mixed Runners-Up Glen Petticrew & Shanti Petticrew
  • Grade 7 - Mixed Winners Ciara O'Donoghue & Gerard Morgan
  • Grade 5 - Mixed Winners Mary Carpenter & Brian Keville
  • Single Mens - 5 Winner Brian Keville
  • Single Mens Senior Winner - Farooq Khalil
  • Senior Mixed Winners - Lynn Boylan (Kilmessan) & Paul Ennis

From all your club mates, Well Done! Check out from the championships in the gallery

On the nights when the championship qualifiers were played at the Claremont Stadium, our members ran a shop in the canteen and we would like to firstly thank our members who helped out and also to Meath Championship players who came in and availed of our services, you help is greatly appreciated!!

Meath Badminton Associations Roberta Hamilton also noted; Many thanks to Shanti Petticrew from Navan Badminton Club who dropped in a magnificent badminton bag shaped cake to Kildlakey Hall last night in time for the players & spectators to enjoy Photo in gallery, well done Shanti

We are still competing on many fronts in our MBA leagues and cups, so keep up the good work!
