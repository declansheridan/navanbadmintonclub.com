15 February 2016 - Meath Championships 2016, Season so far
Meath Championship 2016 - Congratulations to all NBC players who participated in the 2016 MBA Championships. At Finals night on February 12th we had a great representation with the following honours....

  • Senior Mens - Winners - Colm O'Brien & Paul Ennis
  • Grade 5 Ladies Singles - Winner - Wanzhing Lim
  • Grade 5 Ladies Singles - Runner Up - Madge Boyle
  • Grade 5 Mens - Winners - Saddam Khan & Gerard Morgan
  • Grade 5 Mixed - Runners Up - Wanzhing Lim & Saddam Khan
  • Grade 6 Ladies - Winners - Shanti Petticrew & Ciara Boylan
  • Grade 6 Mixed - Runners Up - Ciara Boylan & Will Hogan
  • Grade 6 Mens - Runners Up - Nigel Boyle & Stephen O'Byrne.
  • Grade 8 Mens - Winners - Ram Seeniraj & Bryan Patterson
Congratulations to all players involved in all finals on the night, it was a great display of badminton by all players! Check out the gallery for some pictures.

Also during the championship we ran a fundraiser at the Claremont by way of a shop in the canteen. Each member managed to donate some food items or some time to staff the shop, and we would like to thank you all for you assistance and generosity.

Meath Badminton League and Cup - The end of season run-in is well underway, with many teams soon to play their final league fixtures. We have teams still competing for final and semi-final spots, so keep the pressure on! Joe will be in touch with team captains to advise on Cup fixtures.

Meath Badminton Website - Visit the MBA website for news and upcoming events along with the current league tables! http://www.meathbadminton.webs.com

Subscriptions - as the season continues we would ask members to continue pay there subs plenty of options available. Talk with Bernie for more info
