Join Us for Beginner Club Night at Navan Badminton Club

Authors
  • Name
    NBC
    Twitter
    @NavanBC

Well, hold onto your rackets, because Navan Badminton Club is throwing open its doors and rolling out the red carpet (okay, it’s just a gym floor, but work with us here 😉) for our Beginner Club Night at Claremont Stadium between 9pm - 11pm.

If you’re new to badminton, don’t worry—we’re as welcoming as a warm cup of tea on a cold Irish October morning. If you’ve never hit a shuttle, we promise you’ll be smacking it around like a pro in no time. If you’re already a seasoned member, you can come along too.

All 4 courts are available, so there's plenty of space —and let’s face it, smashing a shuttle is as satisfying as finding money in an old coat pocket. So, join us and make Thursday the most shuttle-rific night of the week!

📍 Location: Claremont Stadium

🕘 Time: 9pm - 11pm

Looking forward to seeing you there!
