28 March 2016 - Close to end of season .. lots of badminton still to play!
Reaching end of season - April will be the end of the club season. Plans are under way for a summer club.

Subscriptions - any outstanding subs for this season are now due! Please talk to Bernie to arrange getting them sorted before we finish for the season.

AGM - the clubs AGM will take place on Thursday, 27th April in Claremont @ 8pm. All members are urged to attend as we plan ahead for next season.

Club Gear - our existing supplier has recently closed their Irish operation. The club are currently approaching alternative vendors. Watch out for the new options coming soon!

Lenister Inter-League - We have two teams representing the club and county in the Leinster inter league championship. On Wednesday, 30th March our Grade 6 Mixed Team welcome Nomads BC from Bray, County Wicklow to Navan for their Quarter final, they hope to repeat the success of our Grade 5 Mixed Team who squeezed past Turin BC, Mullingar of the Midlands League last week. The finals and semi finals will be held in Baldoyle/Terenure on Sunday, 10th April
