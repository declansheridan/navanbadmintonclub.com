05 October 2013 - Season well under way, coaching, league preparations, tournaments
Some great club nights already this year with good crowds on Mondays and Wednesdays with good enjoyable badminton!!

The Meath league kick off is fast approaching and the deadline for entry of teams is soon. So if you haven't let Dermot know that your interested in playing on a team let him know as soon as possible.

Coaching has been arranged again if you haven't heard about it get in touch with Dermot, the plan is to have a beginners group with Martin on a Monday from 8:30pm to 9:30pm. And for the those with more experience we hope to organise groups with Colm (who will try and sort out the many years of bad habits for us hopefully :) ) on a Wednesday night, details to be confirmed.

With the league and season about to really go into full swing a gentle reminder that any start on paying your membership fees greatly appreciated flexible approaches on how to pay just talk with Elaine or Dermot

Note: Juvenile Badminton also back full time on Mondays. Kicks off @ 7:00pm

Also were trying to gauge if there is any interest with any surrounding local clubs to having a Saturday morning knock up. Not a club night but would be a pay as you go. If your interested follow up on facebook see below.
