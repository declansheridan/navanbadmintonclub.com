08 June 2014 - End of Season, Summer Badminton, Club AGM / Night out, Winners and Awards
Summer Badminton - For those who can't live without Badminton during the summer. There will be a summer club in Claremont every Saturday morning between 10am - 12pm. It is 5 Euro for all MBA and BI affiliated members pay on the day.

Club AGM / Night Out - The only way to build on the successful year is to start to get organised for next season! The plan is to hold the Club AGM on Friday, 27th June. We will hold the AGM followed by a night night we would encourage all members to try and make it so they can contribute to the season ahead. Venue yet to be confirmed please keep an eye out for update.

End of season - Well done! to all members on making it a remarkable 2013 / 2014 season for the club!! A club is only as strong as its members. And all members contributed to a great season for the club from long standing existing members to new, through all the different grades. And proof of it was at the MBA (Meath Badminton Association) Awards Dinner Dance when we won ....

2013 / 2014 Club of the Year



... it was great for us to receive the award with so many good clubs in contention. It may even have been our first time winning it as a club according to our resident oracle Joe! Also on the night it was great to have Madge Boyle awarded the special merit award by MBA for her contributions to badminton in Meath a real worthy and deserved winner.
