30 May 2016 - Season finishes on high note!!
Meath Badminton Club of the year.
Club of the year - An amazing way to cap off a great year for the club. Thanks to all members for there contributions during the year which resulted in the club being presented with Meath club of the year for the 2nd time in 3 years!!! Its all the hard work and sportsmanship of members both on and off the court that earned the club the award.



Player of the year - Ciara Boylan - Big congratulations to Ciara who was awarded Meath Badminton Player of the year. Well deserved having played some great badminton all season.

On the court - We had a great season with many teams very competitive in there grades and club nights well attended giving a great social side to the club. Full list of honours for the season below:

  • Grade 5 Ladies League - Winners - Navan A - Edel Matthews, Madge Boyle, Bernie O Reilly, Wanzhing Wim
  • Grade 5 Ladies Cup - Winners - Navan A - Edel Matthews, Madge Boyle, Bernie O Reilly, Wan Zhing Linn
  • Grade 5 Mens League - Winners - Navan - Hingwah Lee, Saddam Khan, Dermot Crowley, Gerard Morgan
  • Grade 5 Mixed Cup - Runners up - Navan A - Dermot Crowley, Dermot Brennan, Madge Boyle, Imelda Booth
  • Grade 6 Mixed League - Winners - Navan B - Ali Shah, Kyle Dunphy, Ciara Boylan, Katie Boland
  • Grade 6 Mixed Cup - Winners - Navan B - Ali Shah, Kyle Dunphy, Ciara Boylan, Katie Boland

  • Grade 6 Ladies League - Runners Up - Navan A - Ciara Boylan, Katie Boland, Aine Crummey, Shanti Petticrew
Summer Badminton - and if you just can't live without Badminton during the Summer. There is a summer club up and running every Wednesday in Claremont @ 8pm. 5 Euro for the night open to all.
