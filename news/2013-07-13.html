13 July 2013 - AGM and 600 Club Draw held, Season review, Preparation for 2013/2014 season.
AGM - The Club AGM was held on 21st June followed by the 600 draw (list of winnners below) and night out. Great turnout from members for the AGM followed up by a great night out celebrating the successful season. Plans are under way for the 2013/2014 season kicking off Monday 2nd September 2013 keep an eye on the website for more details.

We also bid a fond farewell to Katharina Schulte who has been with us since September 2012 and played a great season of badminton with our Ladies Grade 5 team. Good Luck to Kathi as she returns home to continue her studies.
