13 July 2013 - Some early details for next season 2012/2013.
We will commence our regular club nights again in September at Claremont, on the following nights:
  • Monday 3rd September 2012
  • Monday 10th September 2012
  • Monday 17th September 2012
  • Wednesday 19th September 2012
  • Monday 24th September 2012
  • Wednesday 26th September 2012
We are hoping to increase our membership numbers from last year, so put the word out amongst your friends and families! During September we will operate a 5 Euro per night 'Pay as you Go' option for prospective members!

Simonstown Badminton Club are running a Summer Club at St. Patricks Secondary School each Wednesday night during July and August from 8pm till 10pm only 5 Euro at the door!!

Join us on Facebook and we can be followed on Twitter. Find the links above.

Our new website is here, more details to be added. Any ideas or suggestions welcome.

The Committee.
