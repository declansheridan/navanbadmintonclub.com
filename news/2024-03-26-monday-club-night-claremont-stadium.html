Monday Club Night at Claremont Stadium

Authors
  • Name

Event Overview

Club night from 9 PM to 11:30 PM, featuring two courts for member use and two scheduled matches commencing at 8:30 PM.

Match Details

  • Senior Men's Division: Navan vs. Kildalkey
  • Grade 5 Men's Division: Navan 'A' vs. Turin

Juvenile Club Night

The evening will also host our Juvenile Club Night. The girls' session will begin at 6:30 PM, followed by the boys' session at 7:30 PM.

Please be advised that the Juvenile Boys' session has reached full capacity, and we are unable to accept new members at this time.
