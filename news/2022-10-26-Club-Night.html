Wednesday's Adult Club Night - 8pm to 11pm

We have earmarked 1 court for coaching, just like previous weeks. It consists of two 45-minute coaching sessions.

All courts are open between 8pm to 8.30pm and 10pm to 11pm.

The evening also features Juvenile Club Night. It's the juvenile boys' turn this week between 6.30pm and 8pm.
