RESULTS

Monday, 8th Apr:

  • Snr Mixed: Navan 'B' 2-7 Athboy/Kells CUP SEMI-FINAL

Navan 'B' faced off against Athboy/Kells in the Cup Semi-Final and despite a strong effort, the scoreboard wasn't exactly on our side, ending with a 7-2 loss.

Tuesday, 9th Apr:

  • G4 Ladies: Navan 3-0 WSBC-LEAGUE FINAL

  • G6 Ladies: Ardee 4-0 Navan - LEAGUE SEMI-FINAL

Wednesday, 10th Apr:

  • G5 Mixed: Trim 'B' 5-7 Navan 'B' - CUP SEMI-FINAL

In a match that had spectators at the edge of their seats, Navan 'B' clashed against Trim 'B' in the Cup Semi-Final. The scoreboard sawed back and forth, but in the end, Navan 'B' emerged victorious with a thrilling 7-5 triumph!

Thursday, 11th Apr:

  • G3 Ladies: Navan 2-2 New Dawn - LEAGUE PLAYOFF (Navan won 5-4 on sets)

Thursday served up a double dose of badminton drama! In the Grade 3 Ladies League Playoff against New Dawn, it was a nail-biting affair that ended in a 2-2 draw. But wait, the drama didn't stop there as Navan clinched a 5-4 win on sets, securing a spot in the League Final!

  • Snr Ladies: New Dawn 8-4 Navan 'A' - CUP

Meanwhile, in the Senior Ladies Cup, New Dawn managed to defeat a valiant Navan 'A' team in a 8-4 win, setting the stage for an electrifying rematch in the League Final on Monday.
