Navan Badminton Club

Welcome to Navan Badminton Club located in Navan, Co. Meath. It holds its club night every Monday and Wednesday during the season which runs from September to April. Caters for Adults and Juniors of all standards from beginner to advanced.

Club News


13th July 2012 - Some early details for next season.


We will commence our regular club nights again in September at Claremont, on the following nights:

  • Monday 3rd September
  • Monday 10th September
  • Monday 17th September
  • Wednesday 19th September
  • Monday 24th September
  • Wednesday 26th September
We are hoping to increase our membership numbers from last year, so put the word out amongst your friends and families! During September we will operate a €5 per night 'Pay as you Go' option for prospective members!

Simonstown Badminton Club are running a Summer Club at St. Patricks Secondary School each Wednesday night during July and August from 8pm till 10pm only €5 at the door!!

Join us on Facebook and we can be followed on Twitter. Find the links above.
