Navan Badminton Club

Welcome to Navan Badminton Club located in Navan, Co. Meath. It holds its club night every Monday and Wednesday during the season which runs from September to April. Caters for Adults and Juveniles of all standards from beginner to advanced.

Juveniles - Some changes to times ....

Dear Parents,

Firstly Happy New year and hope you all had a nice break over the Xmas. Due to increased juvenile numbers this season, the newer members are not getting enough basic coaching while existing juvenile members are not getting enough game & court time. With this in mind, we have decided on the following changes with effect from next Monday 9th January. We have taken an extra half hour so start time will be 6.30pm.

  • Monday nights: 6.30 to 7.30pm - all new members that started this year
  • Monday nights: 7.30 to 8.30pm - all existing juvenile members
  • Thursday nights: 6.30 to 8pm - Juvenile Matches.
This should leave a situation whereby all players are importantly getting more coaching and more game & court time. This should improve things all round. Looking forward to seeing you all back next Monday and do not hesitate to contact either Martin (087)2669648 or Elaine if you want to discuss this matter.

Regards,
Martin, Elaine, Paudge & Declan,
