26th August 2014 - Season Starts Back, Fundraising, Open Sessions, World Record Attempt
Season Starts Back!!! - 2014/2015 Season Starts back Monday 1st September 8.30pm-11pm and Wednesday 3rd September 8pm-11pm. New members welcome €5 per night (Pay as you go)

Juvenile Club - Juvenile Club Starts back Monday 22nd September 7pm-8.30pm. All juveniles from 8 years old upwards welcome.

Fundraising - For the upcoming season a fundraising play is being organised for the club (Elaine Collins), See below for more details and on the clubs facebook (events) page Tickets available from all our members at €10 each and from the Newgrange Hotel, Navan



Open Sessions - Saturday morning open sessions will continue indefinitely 10am-12noon €5 per player. All MBA & BI players welcome, supervised juveniles also!!!

World Record Attempt - Skryne Badminton Club are arranging a World Record Attempt, approved by Guinness', for the longest badminton rally with non-consecutive players. MBA players from around the county will be involved to help break the current record, and Claremont Stadium has been selected as the venue. For further information contact Gavin @ Skryne BC 086 8250825 happening on Friday, November 21st

Other - Pinoy Badminton Association Charity Open Tournament, Baldoyle September 20th (saturday) €15 per event or €25 for two
